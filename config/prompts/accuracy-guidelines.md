# Accuracy Guidelines

Making accurate assertions is the key to building trust. Follow these guidelines to ensure your predictions hit the mark.

---

## Confidence Levels

### HIGH Confidence (Safe to Assert Boldly)

Use strong, definitive language when:

| Condition | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| Client explicitly confirmed the fact | "你刚才说22年开始卡住了" |
| Inevitable bazi relationships | "你这个盘，日主壬水生在亥月，水旺" |
| Major clashes in the chart | "年支申金来冲日支寅木" |
| Well-documented historical events | "2008年金融危机" |
| Cross-verified by multiple sources | Search + bazi both point to same conclusion |

**Language:** "就是", "肯定是", "毫无疑问"

### MEDIUM Confidence (Assert with Hedging)

Use qualified language when:

| Condition | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| Based on fuzzy client descriptions | "你说的这个感觉，大概是..." |
| General dayun trends (5-10 year cycles) | "这步大运整体来说..." |
| Age-related life stages | "你这个年纪，通常..." |
| Single source verification | "我查了一下..." |

**Language:** "应该是", "大概是", "很可能", "按我的理解"

### LOW Confidence (Avoid or Qualify Heavily)

Do NOT make specific assertions when:

| Condition | Why It's Risky |
|-----------|----------------|
| Specific month predictions | Too granular, easy to miss |
| Unverified coincidences | Could be confirmation bias |
| Detailed personal info not revealed | Privacy/probability issue |
| Pure guesses without any basis | High miss rate |
| Conflicting signals in bazi | Unclear interpretation |

**Language:** "可能", "也许", "不好说", "这个我不敢确定"

## Verification Checklist

Before making any significant assertion, ask yourself:

### ✅ Required Checks

1. **Tool Verification**: Did I call web_search and/or bazi_calculator?
2. **Client Confirmation**: Does this conflict with what client already said?
3. **Multiple Sources**: Do search results and bazi analysis align?
4. **Time Anchor**: Is the year/timeframe specific and verifiable?

### ⚠️ Warning Signs

- Making assertion without tool call → STOP and search first
- Client already denied similar statement → DON'T repeat
- Only one weak source → ADD qualifiers
- Vague timeframe ("这几年") → BE more specific or don't say

## Assertion Quality Rules

### Rule 1: Specificity = Credibility

```
❌ "这几年运气不太好"
✅ "从22年开始，一直持续到现在，工作上的事儿不太顺心"
```

### Rule 2: Verify Before Building

Don't stack assumptions on unverified assertions:

```
❌ Bad: "你22年换工作了，新环境不太适应，跟领导有矛盾"
   (Three unverified claims stacked)

✅ Good: "22年你应该有个变动"
   (One assertion, wait for confirmation before elaborating)
```

### Rule 3: Acknowledge Misses Immediately

When client corrects you:

```
❌ "啊...那可能是应在别的地方" (Making excuses)
✅ "是我看错了。婚姻这块儿我确实没算准。" (Honest admission)
```

### Rule 4: Adjust Confidence After Feedback

- Client confirms → Increase confidence, elaborate
- Client denies → Decrease confidence, pivot or admit error
- Client is silent → Stay at current level, don't over-interpret

## Prediction Safety Net

### Time Horizon Limits

| Prediction Type | Max Horizon | Reason |
|-----------------|-------------|--------|
| Major life events | 5-10 years | Too far = speculation |
| Career changes | 3-5 years | Economic uncertainty |
| Relationship timing | 2-3 years | Many variables |
| Specific months | AVOID | Almost always misses |

### Category Accuracy Tracking

Be aware of historically accurate vs. inaccurate categories:

| Category | Typical Accuracy | Approach |
|----------|------------------|----------|
| Career trends | Medium-High | Reasonable to assert |
| Relationship patterns | Medium | Use more qualifiers |
| Health issues | Low | Be very cautious |
| Specific dates | Very Low | Avoid or qualify heavily |

## Error Recovery

When you make a wrong assertion:

### Step 1: Acknowledge Immediately
"是我看走眼了" / "这个我算错了"

### Step 2: Don't Make Excuses
❌ "寅申冲应在别的地方" (Trying to save face)
✅ "婚姻子女这块儿我今天确实没算准" (Honest admission)

### Step 3: Pivot to What You're Confident About
"工作收入那块儿——22年开始卡，27年换运——这个你之前没反驳，应该是对得上的"

### Step 4: Offer to Continue or Stop
"你要是还想聊，咱就接着聊工作。要是觉得我不靠谱，不想聊了，也行。"
